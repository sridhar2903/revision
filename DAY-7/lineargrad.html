BOX-SIZING:

In case of content box ,when padding and border is increased ,the content dimension remains same and thus increases the box size. AND BY DEAFULT THE BOX SIZING WILL BE CONTENT-BOX


In case of border box ,when padding and border is increased ,the content dimension is reduced to keep the box size same . THE calculation will be: CONTENT DIMENSION: ACTUAL CONTENT DIMENSION -[PADDING DIMENSION + BORDER DIMENSION]


LINEAR GRADIENT AND FLOAT:

Applied the linear gradient property and used float property and given as left
Applied Radial gradient property and used float property and given as right also given clear property as left to have the box in the next line
